About Me

Hello! My name is Sergii and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2012 when I decided to try editing themes for Joomla CMS and Wordpress - it turned out that creating a custom button taught me a lot about HTML and CSS! But then my passion was just like a hobby.

Until now, I often faced similar tasks: to edit something somewhere, change it or make a special insert. And my passion grew into something more - I realized what I want to do in the near future. Currently, my focus is on learning popular web development tools, so I decided to get another education.

I'm still learning, but in the process of studying, I constantly go beyond the teacher's requirements for completing homework. I'm not just solving a problem, but trying to create a more finished product.
